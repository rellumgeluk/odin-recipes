Tapioca

Ingredients:

  1. 1 cup hydrated tapioca starch
  2. 1 or 2 slices of cheese
  3. Salt
  4. 1 teaspoon of butter
  5. 1 or 2 slices of ham

    6. Steps:

    1. Sift the ready-made tapioca starch
    2. Place in a frying pan and spread well until it covers the entire pan
    3. Place it in a frying pan over medium heat and, when it starts to come away from the edges, turn the tapioca over
    4. As soon as it turns, spread the butter
    5. Add the filling and fold
    6. Turn to brown all sides and it's ready
