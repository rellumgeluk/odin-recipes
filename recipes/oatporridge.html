Oat Porridge (cold)

Ingredients:

  1. Protein powder
  2. Rolled oats
  3. Chia seeds
  4. Banana
  5. Milk
  6. Peanut butter (optional)

    7. Steps:

    1. Add all ingredients to a pot on the stove top, and stir to incorporate everything evenly
    2. Fill a bowl or other recipient of choice with mix
    3. Let in the fridge for one hour or more and it's ready
